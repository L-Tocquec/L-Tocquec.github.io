Welcome on my website !

I am currently a first-year doctoral student at the University of Paris-Saclay, affiliated with the Orsay Mathematics Laboratory. My research focuses on Optimal Entropic Transport and its generalizations, under the supervision of Luca Nenna (University of Paris-Saclay) and Paul Pegon (University of Paris-Dauphine). I am also a member of the Inria teams MoKaplan (Inria Paris) and ParMa (Inria Paris-Saclay).

My research interests include optimal transport, calculus of variations, and optimization more broadly.
