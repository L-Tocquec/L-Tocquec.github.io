Welcome on my website !

I am currently a first-year doctoral student at the University of Paris-Saclay, affiliated with the Orsay Mathematics Laboratory. I am also a member of the Inria teams Mokaplan (Inria Paris) and ParMa (Inria Saclay).

My thesis focuses on Optimal Entropic Transport and its generalizations and is supervised by Luca Nenna (Univ. Paris-Saclay) and Paul Pegon (Univ. Paris-Dauphine).
I am particularly interested in optimal transport, calculus of variations and optimization more generally.
