Welcome on my website !

I am currently a first-year doctoral student at the University of Paris-Saclay, affiliated with the Orsay Mathematics Laboratory.
My thesis focuses on Optimal Entropic Transport and its generalizations, under the supervision of Luca Nenna (Univ. of Paris-Saclay) and Paul Pegon (Univ. of Paris-Dauphine).
I am also a member of the Inria teams MoKaplan (Inria Paris) and ParMa (Inria Paris-Saclay).

I am particularly interested in optimal transport, calculus of variations and optimization more generally.
