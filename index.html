Welcome on my website !

I am currently a first-year doctoral student at the University of Paris-Saclay (Orsay Mathematics Laboratory). I am doing my thesis - Optimal Entropic Transport and Some Generalizations - under the supervision of Luca Nenna (University of Paris-Saclay) and Paul Pegon (University of Paris-Dauphine). I am also part of the Inria team MoKaplan (Inria Paris) and ParMa (Inria Paris-Saclay).

I am particularly interested in optimal transport, the calculus of variations, and optimization more generally.
